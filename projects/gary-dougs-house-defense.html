Gary Doug's House Defense

Team project · Unity · 2D pixel animation · Character and background design

Introduction

Gary Doug's House Defense is a 2D arcade game inspired by the classic game Dig Dug, which was given as the starting brief for the project. The story follows a playful old man named Gary Doug, who gets into a snowball fight with a group of naughty kids after they break his window and scare his grandchildren.

The player collects snow tiles while walking to form snowballs and throw them at the kids. Meanwhile, the children move around the map and chase the player, keeping the pace energetic and playful.

My part

This game was created through teamwork. Another teammate and I were responsible for the art, while the other members focused on programming and sound design.

My contributions focused on character design, animation, and UI.

Character design

I chose an old man as the main playable character to create an unexpected contrast with the mischievous kids and make the setting more memorable. I first created a rough 96-pixel version of Gary Doug, then refined the design into a cleaner and more polished 32-pixel sprite.

I revised our first version of the kids to feel more cheeky and to promote empathy toward the old man instead of the children. I removed their eyes to reduce detail and make them feel more mischievous and stylized.

Animations

I created all character animations using Piskel, a browser-based tool for pixel art. To match the game's tone, I leaned into a cute and childlike visual language, amplifying facial expressions and body poses to exaggerate personality.

  • Gary walk animation
  • Gary throwing snowball animation
  • Angry Gary expression
  • Winning animation with Gary's dancing poses
  • Kids' throwing snowball animation
  • Kids' defeated animation

Environment & UI design

To support the characters, I created decorative background elements to make the environment feel lively and energetic. I also contributed to the menu screen and start screen:

  • Background designs that frame the snowball fight in a readable, fun layout.
  • A menu scene set inside Gary's house, where players can adjust the volume. The scene shows Gary's daughter cooking.
  • A start screen that briefly introduces the story and the basic gameplay before the player enters the main level.
